Why is Envoy sending 404s to CONNECT requests?¶

Envoy’s default matchers match based on host and path. Because CONNECT requests (generally) do not have a path, most matchers will fail to match CONNECT requests, and Envoy will send a 404 because the route is not found. The solution for HTTP/1.1 CONNECT requests, is to use a connect_matcher as described in the CONNECT section of the upgrade documentation.
